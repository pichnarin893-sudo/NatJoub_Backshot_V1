## 1. Overview

## 2. Cancellation Request Process

### 2.1 How to Request a Cancellation

To cancel your booking and request a refund:

1. **Log in** to your NatJoub account
2. Navigate to **"My Bookings"**
3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
4. Click **"Request Cancellation"**
5. Provide a **reason for cancellation** (optional but recommended)
6. Submit your request

### 2.2 Owner Approval Required

**All cancellation requests require approval from the property owner.** Your refund is not guaranteed and is subject to the owner's decision. The owner will review your request and:

- ‚úÖ **Approve** - Your booking will be cancelled and refund processed
- ‚ùå **Reject** - Your booking remains active and no refund is issued

You will receive a real-time notification when the owner makes their decision.

### 2.3 Minimum Cancellation Window

**Cancellation requests must be submitted at least 2 hours before your check-in time.**

- ‚úÖ **Allowed:** Requesting cancellation 2+ hours before check-in
- ‚ùå **Not Allowed:** Requesting cancellation less than 2 hours before check-in
- ‚ùå **Not Allowed:** Requesting cancellation after check-in time has passed

## 5. Refund Processing Timeline

### 5.1 Owner Review Period

Property owners have **unlimited time** to review your cancellation request. We encourage owners to respond promptly, but there is no automatic approval deadline.

### 5.2 Refund Processing Time

Once your cancellation is **approved by the owner**:

- **Refund Initiated:** Immediately upon approval
- **Refund Visible in Account:** 5-7 business days
- **Bank Account Credit:** 7-10 business days (depending on your bank)

### 5.3 Refund Method

Refunds are processed through the **same payment method** used for the original booking. If you paid via:

- **Credit/Debit Card:** Refund to the same card
- **Digital Wallet:** Refund to the same wallet
- **Bank Transfer:** Refund to the same bank account

## 16. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Can I cancel my booking?
**A:** Yes, if you submit your cancellation request at least 2 hours before check-in. However, approval from the property owner is required.

### Q2: How long does the owner have to respond?
**A:** There is no time limit. Owners can review requests at their discretion, though we encourage prompt responses.

### Q3: What if the owner doesn't respond?
**A:** Contact our support team if the owner hasn't responded within a reasonable time, and we will follow up.

### Q4: Can I withdraw my cancellation request?
**A:** No. Once submitted, only the owner can approve or reject. Contact the owner directly if you change your mind.

### Q5: Why was my cancellation rejected?
**A:** Owners may reject for various reasons including timing, rebooking difficulty, or their own cancellation policy. Check the rejection reason provided.

### Q6: Can I get a full refund?
**A:** Yes, if you cancel more than 48 hours before check-in AND the owner approves your request.

### Q7: What happens to my promo code?
**A:** If your cancellation is approved, your promo code usage is restored and you can use it again.

### Q8: How is the refund calculated?
**A:** Based on time before check-in (see Section 3.1), minus a 2% gateway fee.
